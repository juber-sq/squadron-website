Our Engineering Services

Custom PCB Design

Professional design of single-layer, double-layer, and high-density multi-layer PCBs, optimized for signal integrity, thermal dissipation, and manufacturability.

Embedded Solutions

Microcontroller board architecture design and optimized low-level firmware engineering (C/C++) for critical real-time performance and absolute reliability.

Product Development

Complete product engineering roadmap support spanning structural conceptualization, physical component sourcing, functional assembly, and volume production.

R&D Services

Comprehensive feasibility research, sensor characterization, circuit testing, design validation, and hardware innovation support for complex patents.

Product Design

Detailed electronic hardware engineering coupled with physical enclosure modeling and mechanical component integration for seamless fit and finish.

IoT Solutions

Cloud-connected smart telemetry devices, sensor mesh networks, wireless protocols (WiFi, BLE, LoRa, cellular), and real-time remote monitoring systems.
